Kenya

Kenya, located in East Africa, is known for its diverse landscapes ranging from savannas to the Great Rift Valley and lakes. Its capital, Nairobi, is a bustling metropolis, while its coastline along the Indian Ocean offers pristine beaches. Kenya is renowned for its wildlife and national parks like Maasai Mara, home to the annual wildebeest migration. The country's rich cultural heritage is reflected in its vibrant traditions and cuisine.
